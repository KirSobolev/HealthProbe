About

HealthProbe is meant to be a helpful tool for healthcare professionals to monitor wellbeing and safety of elderly people and people with special need so that they can live on their own for longer period without nursing home.

In HealthProbe there will be a camera to monitor patients in case of alert, so it is possible to confirm by human that readings are correct. HealthProbe has humidity and thermal monitor so that patients living conditions can be monitored so we can avoid general health issues like getting cold or too hot, or for example to prevent asthmatic people from suffering too dry air quality.

Sound sensor is meant to inform about possible accident that happened to patient, so it captures quick and loud noise that the sensor can detect and forward an alert to be assessed via camera by the healthcare professional to be confirmed if something happened to patient.
